Flash Floods & Water Logging
  • Before: Move electronics and cargo to upper floors. Store clean water.
  • During: Avoid wading or driving through flood currents (as little as 6 inches can knock you off balance). Seek high ground.
  • After: Avoid contact with electrical wiring. Disinfect flooded surfaces and boil drinking water.
Landslides & Mud Creeps
  • Before: Inspect slopes for structural cracks, tilting fences, or saturated spots. Prepare a go-bag.
  • During: If mudflow is imminent, evacuate laterally to a flat plateau. Listen for rumbling sounds.
  • After: Stay away from slide zones. Secondary slips are highly common after the initial landslide.
Cyclones & Severe Winds
  • Before: Tape glass windows in a criss-cross pattern. Secure loose tin roofs or outdoor tools.
  • During: Unplug power connections. Do not venture out during wind lulls, as the eye is followed by severe counterwinds.
  • After: Stay inside until local authorities declare a safe state. Check for water contamination.
Extreme Heat & Sandstorms
  • Before: Keep thick curtains closed to insulate rooms. Seal water storage tanks from sand.
  • During: Stay indoors. Drink saline water (ORS) hourly. Protect animals from direct heat.
  • After: Inspect solar panels and clean accumulated sand dust to restore energy flow.
